Publisher

Turn a research concept into a unique, persona-voiced single-page webpage. A harness wraps a writing agent with four separate pillars that guarantee the output captures the persona's voice and design, is well-researched, and meets a quality bar — otherwise it stops and asks. The agent just writes; constraint-handling is invisible to it.
